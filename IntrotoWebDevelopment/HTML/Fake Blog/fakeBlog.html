Joe owned a zoo with over 80 big cats and other exotic animals, Joe was a country singing artist; you can listen to one of his tracks here, and Joe ran for Governor of Oklahoma as a Liberian candidate. Why do I keep using the past tense? Because Joe no longer has any of these things.
